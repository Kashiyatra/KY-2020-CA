About Us

Kashiyatra, the annual socio-cultural fest of IIT-BHU is a three-day fest held in Banaras, the cultural hub of India. Kashiyatra is known to witness numerous artists flying from all over the world and becomes home for participants from throughout the country during winters. From breathtaking dances, spellbinding musical performances, astounding art pieces, mind-boggling debates and a lot more, the three-day extravaganza sends a wave of hysteria and zeal all over the campus of IIT-BHU. Kashiyatra sees a huge participation from all over the country. With a footfall of 60,000 and more than 600 colleges participating, Kashiyatra is breaking new grounds every year.

Why become a Campus Ambassador ?

Why CA ?

For bringing together a fest which encircles diversity like never before we are reaching out to colleges across the country. The selected Campus Ambassador in his/her tenure will represent Kashiyatra, IIT(BHU) in his/her college and will become a an integral member of the team.

  • Become a Leader
  • Update your organisational skills
  • Polish your communicating & public speaking skills.
  • Get the exposure to online and media marketing
  • Expand your creative horizons
Become a Campus Ambassador

Duties of the CA

CA is the face of Kashiyatra. They act as the link between Kashiyatra and their college and are the first point of contact for the students. Following are the responsibilities one is required to take:

Represent

Represent and exemplify Kashiyatra in your college among your peers.

Publicize

Promote Kashiyatra by publicizing its events through social media platforms.

Conceptualize

Bring forth ideas and proposals as valuable input for team Kashiyatra.

Organize

Conduct workshops and promotional events in your college for Kashiyatra.
